Harshitha - The Silent Prodigy

Endhukante maa idhari madhya communication chaala thakkuva.
Asalu 2 years avuthundhi paiga same lab ayina okkasari kuda matladaledhu ante naake shocking ga vundhi.
Nijaniki thanaku communication skills chaala ekkuva, but aa skill ni thanu ekkuvaga edndhuku use chesukodho thelidhu.

Good Habits:
  • Thanu friendship ki value isthundhi.
  • Thanu emaina work chesetappudu evarini disturb cheyyadhu.
  • Thanaku listening skills kuda ekkuve, cheppindhi ventane artham chesukuntundhi.
Bad Habits:
  • Thanaku communication skills chaala unnai, so vaatini thanu implement chesthene better ani naa feeling.
