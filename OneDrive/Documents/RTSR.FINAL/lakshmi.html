Asalu class loo evaru neeku adagakunna kani help chesedhi ante naku first gurthukochedhi Lakshmi.
Endhukante nenu chala sarlu observe chesannu adhentante, normal ga imadhya aduguthe kuda help cheyaru but alantidhi oka friend adakundaane help chesthundhante nenu ika thanani enthala pogadalani.
Asalu class chebuthunte intresting ga evaraina vintaara ante Lakshmi hee.
Andharu seminar tension tho chebuthe thanu mathram enjoy chesukuntu chebuthundhi.

Good habits:
-> Thanu andhariki help cheylani alochisthundhi.
-> Thanaku listening skills kuda ekkuve.
-> Ante cheppindhi ventane artham chesukuntundhi.
-> Thanu friendship ki entha value isthundho studies ki kuda anthe value isthundhi.
-> Boys ani girls ani difference chupadhu, andharini equal ga chusthundhi.
-> Thanu chaala patience person, endhukante thanani enni sarlu notes adigina kuda konchem kuda chiraku padadhu.
-> Andharithonu comedy ga and friendly ga move avuthundhi.
-> Thanu Face-to-Face person.

Bad habits:

Nick name:- "HELPFUL SPIRIT" (means:- her helpful, friendly, and patient nature that brightens the classroom atmosphere).
