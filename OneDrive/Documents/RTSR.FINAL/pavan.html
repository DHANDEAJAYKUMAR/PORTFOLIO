Naaku class loo first friend ayyindhi evarante Pavan hee.
First year loo chaala close gaa vunde vaadu ante lunch kalise chevaallam, class loo kuda oke bench loo kurchunevallam.
But sudden emaindho thelidhu, aa friendship 2nd year loo maayam ayyindhi, ika 3rd year loo motham antharinchipoyindhi.
Ippatiki thanatho first year laaga friendship cheyyalne vuntundhi but thanu naaku antha touch loo vunde vaadu kadhu.
Thanu studies ki entha preference isthaado, friendship ki kuda anthe preference isthaadu.
But imadhya thanu class ki ekkuva ravatledhu.
Thanu 2nd year loo leader ga vunnaappudu, thanu correct ga responsibility thisukunnaadu, ante evariki problems rakunda chukunnaadu.
Aa leadership skills enta baaga use chedante, malli thane leader ga vundalani class loo more than half members thanane korukunnaru.
Ante thanu responsibilities ni entha correct ga use chesukuntaado thelusthundhi.
Thanu academics lone kadhu, extra activities loo kuda mundhe vuntaadu.
Nijaniki thanaku leadership qualities chaala ekkuve.

Good Things:

  • Thanu andhariki help cheyalane alochisthaadu.
  • Listening skills kuda ekkuve.
  • Thanu eppudaina kotha vishayalu nerchukovadaniki try chesthaadu.
  • Friends thone kadhu faculties tho kuda friendly ga vuntaadu.
  • Boys ani girls ani difference chupinchadu, andharini equal ga chusthaadu.
  • Thanu edhaina thappu chesthe aa thappunu oppukuntaadu.
  • Okavela ee thappu cheyyakapothe mathram thala vanchadu.
  • Emaina kavalante mathram entha kaina thegisthaadu.

Bad Things:

  • 2nd year loo thanu leadership skills ni correct ga use chesukunnadu, so aa skills ippudu kuda malli use chesukunte inka top vuntaadu ani naa feeling.

Nick Name:

"Dynamic Leader Pavan" (means: Reflecting his strong leadership qualities and ability to inspire and manage effectively!)
