Nijaniki nenu eppudu thanatho comedy ga and friendly ga vundadanike try chesthaanu.

Asalu class loo evari voice chinnapillalaaga vasthundhi ani aduguthe, naaku first gurthukochedhi malli Raji hee.

Thanu cool ga vunnappudu entha baaga matladuthundho, alage thanu angry or fire loo vunnappudu kuda antha baaga thiduthundhi.
